Privacy Policy

1. Responsible Party

This website is a private, non-commercial fan project (ynnk99). Privacy-related inquiries can be submitted via the Twitch or Discord channel.

2. What this site is

The Elden Ring Boss Tracker is a publicly accessible overlay tool that visualizes progress through a playthrough of Elden Ring. The data is loaded live from Google Sheets and updated every five seconds. No user data is stored on our own servers.

3. Google Analytics

With your consent, this site uses Google Analytics 4, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC, 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA. In doing so, your IP address is transmitted to Google servers in the USA and processed there in anonymized form. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect usage statistics (e.g. page views, time on page). Data transfer to the USA takes place on the basis of the EU Standard Contractual Clauses.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent).
You can withdraw your consent at any time via the "Withdraw consent" link in the cookie banner, which is shown again after withdrawal.

More information: Google Privacy Policy  ·  Google Analytics Opt-out

4. Google Sheets (data retrieval)

The boss data (progress, deaths, times) is loaded regularly via HTTP request from a publicly shared Google Spreadsheet, once you have given consent in the cookie banner. In doing so, your IP address is transmitted to Google LLC, USA, as part of normal HTTP communication. No further personal data is stored or processed from this retrieval. No external requests are made before you make a decision in the cookie banner.
Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent). You can withdraw your consent at any time via the "Reset cookie consent" button at the end of this privacy policy.

5. Twitch login (optional editor access)

Certain editing features require a login via Twitch OAuth. In this process you are redirected to the Twitch Interactive, Inc. (USA) website. After successful authentication, the site stores your Twitch access token and Twitch username exclusively in your browser's localStorage – no data is transmitted to our own servers. This data is deleted when you log out, or remains until you delete it manually.
Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent through active login).

6. Local storage (localStorage)

This site uses your browser's localStorage to store your cookie consent and, if applicable, your Twitch token. This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to other websites.

7. Hosting

This site is hosted via GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc., USA). GitHub may log technical access data (IP address, timestamp) in the process. More information: GitHub Privacy Statement.

8. Your rights

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, and restrict the processing of your data, as well as the right to data portability. Complaints can be filed with the competent data protection authority (in Germany: the relevant State Commissioner for Data Protection).
